REFUND POLICY


Cancellation of Rental Lockers

If you decide to cancel your locker rental booking 30 mins before the scheduled time slot, you are eligible for a full refund.

Contact our customer support team as soon as possible to initiate the cancellation process.

Refunds will be processed using the same payment method used for the original transaction.



Damaged or Defective Lockers

In the rare event that you receive a damaged or defective locker, please contact our customer support team.

Provide clear pictures of the damaged or defective locker, along with a detailed description of the problem.

We will assess the situation and provide instructions on replacing the locker.

Once we receive the damaged or defective locker, we will either replace it or issue a refund, as per your preference.



Refunds

Refund Eligibility: Refunds are only applicable in the cases mentioned above (damaged or defective lockers, and cancellation).

Refunds will not be provided for cancellations made after the scheduled time slots.

Refund Processing Time: Please allow up to 3 business days for the refund to be processed after we have received the returned locker or confirmed the cancellation.

Refund Method: Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the transaction.

If the original payment method is no longer valid, please contact our customer support team to make alternative arrangements.

Late Cancellation: Refunds for cancellations made after the scheduled slots may be considered on a case-by-case basis and are subject to management approval.

This policy is subject to change, and any updates will be reflected on our website.
